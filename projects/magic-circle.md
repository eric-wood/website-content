I first met Mike from [Fowl Sounds](https://www.fowlsoundsfx.com) at a local pedal show years ago.
Since then we've become fast friends and constantly bounce ideas off each other; there's not a lot of guitar pedal builders in Denver, and having someone nearby to catch up with in person or lend parts was super helpful.

When Mike reached out about adding my switching system to an artist pedal he was working on I couldn't pass up the invitation!
He was designing a multi-effect for [Midwife](https://heavenmetal.bandcamp.com) and needed toggles for each of the three effects as well as "hold for momentary" switch operation.

I made some modifications to my [switching system](https://github.com/heuristic-industries/two-switch) to accommodate a third switch and flash the LEDs when the switches are held in momentary mode.
Overall a simple project with a fantastic payoff.
It was a blast seeing her perform live in a packed theater with an early prototype!

Mike did incredible work on this pedal and the end result sounds incredible.
It's a very complex device with a lot of fancy switching and routing internally that I got to help review.
Fitting all three circuits together on a single PCB and keeping it quiet took a lot of work and patience, and the end result speaks for itself!
